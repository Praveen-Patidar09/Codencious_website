THE CHALLENGE
Limited Access
to Clinics
With clinics closed during
COVID-19,patients missed
critical physiotherapy
sessions— stalling recovery
and progress.
No Visibility Into
Patient Movement
Without in-person visits,
doctors had no way to see
if patients were doing
their exercises correctly
or consistently.
Privacy Barriers
With Cloud Solutions
Sharing video data via cloud
tools raised serious privacy
concerns—posing risks to
patient confidentiality
and compliance.
Codenscious.ai addressed these challenges head-on—with a private, efficient, and remote-first AI solution.
OUR SOLUTION A Smarter, Safer Way to
Support Recovery— Powered by AI Codenscious.ai developed a real-time movement
analysis system that runs entirely on your phone
—no cloud needed, no compromises on privacy.
Just smart, seamless support for physiotherapists
and their patients, right when it’s needed most.
Real-Time Exercise Analysis On-Device & Privacy-First Automated Progress Reports AI tracks posture and movement in real-time,
helping patients follow exercises correctly
—every rep, every frame. All analysis happens right on the phone—
no cloud, no data sharing, no privacy
concerns. Doctors get instant, structured reports on
range, form, and consistency—no need for
manual review.
