Helping Physiotherapists Diagnose Better—With AI On Your Phone

During the pandemic, Z Solutions Canada faced a challenge; enable physiotherapists to monitor recovery without seeing patients in person. Codenscious.ai built an AI-powered tool that analyzes movement—entirely on-device.

THE CHALLENGE

Limited Access to Clinics

With clinics closed during COVID-19, patients missed critical physiotherapy sessions—stalling recovery and progress.

No Visibility Into Patient Movement

Without in-person visits, doctors had no way to see if patients were doing their exercises correctly or consistently.

Privacy Barriers With Cloud Solutions

Sharing video data via cloud tools raised serious privacy concerns—posing risks to patient confidentiality and compliance.

Codenscious.ai addressed these challenges head-on—with a private, efficient, and remote-first AI solution.

OUR SOLUTION

A Smarter, Safer Way to
Support Recovery—Powered by AI

Codenscious.ai developed a real-time movement analysis system that runs entirely on your phone—no cloud needed, no compromises on privacy. Just smart, seamless support for physiotherapists and their patients, right when it's needed most.

Real-Time Exercise Analysis

AI tracks posture and movement in real-time, helping patients follow exercises correctly—every rep, every frame.

Automated Progress Reports

Doctors get instant, structured reports on range, form, and consistency—no need for manual review.

On-Device & Privacy-First

All analysis happens right on the phone—no cloud, no data sharing, no privacy concerns.
